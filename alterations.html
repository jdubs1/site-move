ALTERATIONS and resizing

Most LAGOS bracelets, rings, and necklaces can be custom sized for an individual, comfortable fit. Because each resizing is crafted by hand, the lead time can take up to 3-4 weeks to process. There are two options for you to request an alteration – at the time of purchase or taking your piece to the LAGOS Store.
To provide the best service to you, we are able to begin the process of your alteration and resizing directly at the time of your purchase. At the Sample Sale, Customer Service will be able to answer any questions you might have about our resizing service, estimated timeline of your resizing, and delivery options.
If your Sample Sale purchase is a gift - we strongly recommend keeping your purchase as is for the holiday. Give the jewelry in its purchased standard size – then bring your Sample Sale purchase to the LAGOS Store at 1735 Walnut Street after the holiday to ensure the alteration is made in the correct size.
