WHAT TO EXPECT

FEATURED JEWELRY
The annual LAGOS Sample Sale….First quality jewelry in hundreds of styles representing more than 35 years of LAGOS designs.
PRICING
All jewelry is ticketed with the retail price. At the register purchases will be reduced by fifty percent. And yes – there are taxes in Philadelphia. Please keep in mind that city and state tax totals 8%. This amount is added to your final price.
JEWELRY SELECTION
Jewelry available at the sale changes from year to year. New pieces are available throughout the sale. Supplies and availability of any piece can sellout quickly. We do not hold, lay-away or reserve any pieces – jewelry is sold on a first come first serve basis. All sales are final. We do not take returns on any purchases.
