INSIDER TIPS

• Bring a good book or a good friend – we recommend both! At LAGOS we make every effort to keep your wait time to a minimum. However, given the standard occupancy and seating availability in the room, there could be anywhere from immediate entry to a two hour wait. The receptionist will be able to give you an estimated wait time upon check-in.
• Arrive within the last two hours of the day. Typically these hours are the least crowded and have the shortest wait time for entry into the sample sale.
• We love to hear from you! Please email us at customerservice@lagos.com with any comments you have for us about the sample sale. Any feedback, customer shopping experience or other insider tips you would like to share with us would be great.
